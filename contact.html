Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our auction process, bidding, and more.

How do I place a bid?

To place a bid, you need to create an account and provide payment information. Once registered, navigate to the auction item you're interested in and click the "Bid Now" button. Enter your bid amount (which must be higher than the current bid plus the increment) and confirm your bid.

What happens if I win an auction?

If you win an auction, you'll receive a notification via email and in your account dashboard. You'll need to complete the payment within 48 hours. Once payment is confirmed, shipping arrangements will be made based on the item's details and your location.

How can I sell items on your platform?

To sell items on our platform, you need to register as a seller. Once approved, you can submit your items for auction by providing detailed descriptions, high-quality photos, and setting a reserve price if desired. Our team will review your submission and schedule it for an upcoming auction.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and in some cases, cryptocurrency. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.

How do you verify the authenticity of items?

We have a team of experts who verify the authenticity of items before they're listed for auction. For high-value items, we may require certificates of authenticity or provenance. We also offer a guarantee on all items sold through our platform, ensuring they are as described.
