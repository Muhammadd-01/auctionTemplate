Our Story

From Passion to Platform

Premium Auctions began with a simple idea: create a trusted marketplace where collectors and enthusiasts could find unique items.

Founded in 2010 by a group of collectors who were frustrated with the lack of specialized auction platforms, we started with small local auctions focusing on rare books.

As our reputation for authenticity and fair dealings grew, so did our categories. We expanded into vintage furniture in 2013 and electronic accessories in 2015.

Today, we host hundreds of auctions monthly, connecting thousands of buyers and sellers worldwide through our online platform while maintaining the excitement and transparency of traditional auctions.

Michael Thompson

Founder & CEO
