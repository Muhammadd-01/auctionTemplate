Bidding Tips & Strategies

Expert advice to help you win auctions

Time Your Bids

Wait until the final moments of an auction to place your bid. This strategy, known as "sniping," prevents bidding wars and keeps the final price lower.

Learn more

Research Thoroughly

Before bidding, research the item's market value, condition, and authenticity. Knowledge is power in auctions and helps you avoid overpaying.

Learn more

Set a Budget

Decide on your maximum bid before the auction starts and stick to it. Emotional bidding often leads to overpaying and buyer's remorse.
